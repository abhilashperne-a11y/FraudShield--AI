Fraud Prevention Academy

Learn the 5 critical scam categories in simple language

Scam Type #1

OTP Fraud (ओटीपी धोखाधड़ी)

What is it: Scammers call pretending to be your bank manager, customer support, or electricity board and ask for a 4 or 6 digit One-Time Password sent to your phone.

Crucial Rule: Banks NEVER ask for OTP. Sharing OTP is like handing the keys of your steel locker to a thief.
Scam Type #2

UPI QR Code Scams (क्यूआर कोड घोटाला)

What is it: The fraudster sends you a QR code on WhatsApp saying "Scan this code to receive lottery prize money in your account."

Crucial Rule: Scanning a QR Code and entering your UPI PIN always DEDUCTS money from your account. You NEVER need to enter PIN to receive money!
Scam Type #3

Fake Customer Care Numbers

What is it: When searching Google for customer care numbers of Amazon, Paytm, or courier services, fraud numbers appear. They ask you to install apps like AnyDesk or TeamViewer.

Crucial Rule: These apps allow hackers to see and control your mobile phone. Never install screen-sharing apps on advice of strangers.
